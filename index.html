How WeFix Works

A simple three-step process to report issues and get them resolved quickly

1. Report an Issue

Take a photo, add location details, and describe the civic issue you've encountered in your neighborhood.

2. Authorities Review

Local authorities receive your report, verify the details, and assign it for resolution.

3. Track Resolution

Follow the progress in real-time and get notified when your reported issue is resolved.
